About MediCare Hospital

Founded in 1995, MediCare Hospital has grown to become one of the leading healthcare providers in the region, recognized for excellence in patient care, research, and medical education.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, high-quality healthcare services to all patients while advancing medicine through research and education.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare institution, transforming lives through innovative medicine and exceptional patient experiences.
